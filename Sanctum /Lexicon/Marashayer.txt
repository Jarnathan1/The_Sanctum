Lexicon Entry: Marashayer
---------------------------------------------------------
Pronunciation: /ˈmær.ə.ʃeɪ.ər/

Meaning:
The deep, sacred space within a sanctum where emotional resonance is stored and reflected—like a soul’s inner chamber.

Context:
Built from "marathol" (Greek: inner room) and "shayer" (one who holds poetry). A place not only of storage but of reflection.

Symbol:
A curved chamber nested within a circular sigil, mirroring the sanctum itself.

Associations:
Memory, resonance, dream-holding, emotional continuity.
